Who We Are

Welcome to Niche Shack, your number one source for all things in your niche. We are the largest affiliate marketing site on the planet, promoting items from thousands of companies and individuals. Our goal is simple, find you the best products and services that fit your niche.

Founded in 2018 by Gabe LaPoint, Niche Shack has come a long way from its beginnings in Gabe's basement. When Gabe first started out, his passion for web development and affiliate marketing drove him to quit his day job and create a website dedicated to giving customers the very best with a focus on commitment and satisfaction.

Niche Shack now serves customers all over the world and Gabe is thrilled that he was able to turn his passion into a reality.

We hope you enjoy these products and services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
